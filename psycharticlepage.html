Psychedelic Therapy Article

A magazine spread and website page I wrote, designed, and coded for my Design for Storytelling journalism class.

This article explores the life-changing mental health benefits of LSD, ketamine, and other psychedelic substances and the legal barriers preventing their use in therapy treatment. I wrote the article after interviewing my psychonautic subjects, painted and edited the acid wash cover illustration in Procreate, and brought everything into InDesign for the final magazine spread.

I also coded the web version of this article using HTML and CSS, which can be viewed online here.
