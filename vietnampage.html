Vietnam Infographic Poster

In my Information Design class, I created an infographic poster depicting various statistics about Vietnamese demographics, cultural exports, weather patterns, and more to provide an educational overview of the country.

The project required designing a series of infographics to effectively communicate complex information about an assigned location through concise visuals. I began by drawing the icons in Procreate, then converted them into vector art in Illustrator. Finally, I assembled the graphs and organized each data point in InDesign to create a cohesive and informative poster layout.
