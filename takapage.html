Taka App

This is a UX prototype I designed for my Interaction Design 2: Mobile course.

I created an on-the-go meditation app that incorporates tratakka principles – a form of meditation that involves staring at a fixed point for a sustained duration – to help Northeastern students find moments to center themselves and destress throughout their busy days.
