Font Exploration Book

A typeface and logotype exploration book I designed for my Typography I class.

For this project, I showcased 24 different typefaces by editing U.S. postal stamp designs in Photoshop. I also created 24 different logotypes by combining different letters and symbols in Illustrator. I ultimately organized these exploratory assets and fonts by producing a final book in InDesign.
