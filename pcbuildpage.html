Personal Computer Build Infographic

I designed a how-to guide infographic for my Information Design I class, detailing each step and component involved in building a personal computer.

This assignment asked me to break down a technical step-by-step process into an accessible visual artifact. I began by sketching and coloring each computer part in Procreate, then imported the illustrations into Illustrator for detailed labeling. The final layout was assembled in InDesign, resulting in an informative and visually appealing infographic.
