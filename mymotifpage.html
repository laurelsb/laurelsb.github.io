MyMotif UX Platform

A mobile app prototype, series of web mockups, and social media branding I made for my Design Degree Project course.

MyMotif is a a modern music cataloging platform and social network built for healthy, qualitative discussions over data metrics and decimals. It acts as positive community hub for music hobbyists to organize, review, and discuss the albums they love that's ultimately unified under a clean and modern design aesthetic.
