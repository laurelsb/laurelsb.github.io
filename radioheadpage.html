Radiohead Discography Photo Book

I designed a thematic photo book for my Typography II class centered around Radiohead's discography.

The project aimed to establish a cohesive design system while showcasing the artistic contributions of Stanley Donwood and Thom Yorke. I used Adobe InDesign to create a visually engaging layout that balanced typographic elements with the band's distinctive album art and illustrations. Through this design intervention, I created a streamlined and visually appealing photo book to highlight the intersection of music and graphic design.
