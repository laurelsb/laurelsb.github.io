Psychedelic Therapy Article

In my Design for Storytelling journalism class, I authored and designed a print layout article discussing the mental health benefits of psychedelics. Additionally, I coded a webpage to host the story for digital formats.

This article explores the therapeutic potential of substances like LSD and ketamine while addressing the legal barriers to their use. After conducting interviews with psychonautic subjects, I painted the main cover illustration in Procreate and used InDesign to produce the final magazine spread layout. I also used HTML and CSS to code a corresponding web page in order to enhance reading accessibility and reach. This project highlights my ability to integrate storytelling, graphic design, and web development to produce informative and engaging multimedia content.
