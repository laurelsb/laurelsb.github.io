Macbeth Type Book

In my Typography I course, I designed a type book for the story of Macbeth, channeling a Shakespearean tragedy through typographic design.

I was tasked with creating a typographical system for a movie or play script of my choice. Using Illustrator and InDesign, I developed a cohesive design that combined the text with graphic motifs that reflected the emotional impact and plot of the story. This project showcased my ability to interpret literary themes through graphic design and create a visually compelling typographic system.
