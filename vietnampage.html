Vietnam Infographic Poster

A poster I designed for my Information Design class.

I was tasked with designing a series of infographics for an assigned country. I drew the icons in Procreate and transformed them into vector art with Illustrator. I then pulled these images into InDesign, where I also made and organized the graphs and their associated data points.
