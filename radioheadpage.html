Radiohead Discography Photo Book

This is a photo book I designed for my Typography II class.

I was tasked with creating a photo book with a specific theme and a streamlined design system. I decided to focus my project on different albums in the alternative rock band Radiohead's discography and showcase artwork produced by artist Stanley Donwood and band singer Thom Yorke.
