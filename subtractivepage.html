Subtractive Poster

I created a poster celebrating typographical ligatures and terms for my Typography II course.

The task was to produce a design that defined multiple typographic terms and integrated them into a cohesive visual narrative. Using InDesign for layout and Procreate for initial sketches, I developed a poster that elegantly displayed these terms, enhancing the viewer's understanding and appreciation of typography. This project reinforced my ability to combine educational content with aesthetic design.
