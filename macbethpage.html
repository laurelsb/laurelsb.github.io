Macbeth Type Book

A type book I produced for my Typography I class.

This project challenged me to design a typographial system that would invoke the energy of a choosen script on the page. I decided to tackle the story of Macbeth and used Illustrator and InDesign to channel the famous Shakespherean tragedy through graphic design.
