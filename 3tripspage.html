Three Trips Infographic

An infographic map I designed for my Information Design I class.

For this project, I was asked to create a poster that showcased three different types of trips: traveling from my Boston apartment to Northeastern, my hometown, and an overseas location.

I drew every landmark marker in Procreate and used Illustrator to organize and produce visual assets and data points like travel time. (For a higher res image, click on the poster and zoom in!)
